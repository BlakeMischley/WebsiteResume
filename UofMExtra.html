Haiti.

In 2017, I had the opportunity to travel with a small group from my church to Haiti. We stayed with our sister church's pastor, Father Murat, and visited the local schools. We brought medicine, food, and lots of school supplies. It was an eye-opening experience. Haiti has been struck with so many challenges, whether political instability or natural disasters. Yet, the people there are some of the happiest and most friendly people I have ever met. Interacting with the school children and locals caused great self growth, inspired me, and made me realize how fortunate I am.
